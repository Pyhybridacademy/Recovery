Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recovery services

How much does recovery cost?

We operate on a contingency basis, meaning you only pay a commission if we successfully recover your funds. Our standard commission is 25-30% of recovered amounts, which is significantly lower than industry standards.

How long does recovery typically take?

Recovery timelines vary depending on the complexity of your case. Simple cases may be resolved in 2-4 weeks, while complex crypto or international cases may take 2-6 months. We'll provide regular updates throughout the process.

What types of scams do you handle?

We specialize in cryptocurrency scams, banking fraud, investment scams, forex trading scams, PayPal fraud, wire transfer fraud, and other financial crimes. If you're not sure if we can help, contact us for a free consultation.

Is my information safe with RecoveryPro?

Yes. We use military-grade encryption, maintain strict confidentiality protocols, and comply with all data protection regulations. Your information is only accessed by authorized team members working on your case.

What is KYC verification?

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is a regulatory requirement. We verify your identity with government-issued ID, address proof, and a selfie. This ensures we're working with legitimate victims and complying with anti-fraud laws.

Can I get a refund if you don't recover funds?

Since we work on contingency, if we don't recover funds, you don't pay our commission. However, some processing fees may apply. We'll discuss all costs upfront before you engage our services.

How do I track my case progress?

You'll have access to your personal dashboard showing real-time case status, updates from your assigned agent, and any evidence we've gathered. You'll also receive email notifications for major milestones.

What happens after funds are recovered?

Once funds are recovered, we guide you through the withdrawal process. You can withdraw via bank transfer, cryptocurrency, or other methods. We handle all legal documentation and compliance requirements.
